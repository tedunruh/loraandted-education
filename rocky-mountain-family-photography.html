ROCKY MOUNTAIN PORTRAIT ADVENTURE

There are not many better vacation destinations than the Rocky Mountains of Colorado. Whether you are in Grand Lake, Winter Park, Keystone, Breckenridge, Vail, or Aspen. Lora and Ted will be there to capture your epic family adventure. When you book your Rocky Mountain Family Portrait Experience with us it will be all about your family and the beautiful location you chose to make memories with your family. No matter if you're here for the snow, the awesome Colorado Mountain spring and summer blooms, or some the greatest rivers in the world. Whatever your passion, we will be there to capture your family memories in a place you love to go! Lora and Ted will provide you with an unparralled Rocky Mountain, Colorado Portrait Adventure.

SESSION PLAN

We will chat about the type of scenery and environment you want, and then pick the best place to do just that. We spend so many hours in the Rocky Moutains photographing families, we are sure to find you an amazing spot too!

YOUR SESSION

Sessions typically last up to an hour (longer for extended family sessions) and are relaxed, enjoyable and fun. We try to avoid stict posing and old fashioned direction giving, but we know how to set you up to look your best so we can provide you with both candid and timeless images.

CREATE YOUR ART

We will help you create the art that embodies your family's adventure. Whether it is wall art, albums or digital files. It is just as important to us that we give you tangible products you can hold in your hands, as it is to take incredible photos.
